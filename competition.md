# Competition

Your project is closest to an AI career copilot for developers:

- analyze GitHub and real projects
- match jobs
- prepare for interviews
- generate tailored CV / portfolio

## Main competitors

### Teal / Huntr / Jobright

These are the strongest broad competitors.

Strong sides:
- complete job-search workflow
- polished resume and tracker features
- strong automation and scale

Weak sides:
- generic, not developer-first
- little GitHub-native credibility
- weaker technical personalization

### Final Round AI / Huru

These are the clearest interview-prep competitors.

Strong sides:
- clear mock interview value
- fast feedback
- strong category awareness

Weak sides:
- narrow compared to your product
- not grounded in actual GitHub/project evidence
- easier to copy as a feature

### interviewing.io / Exponent

These are the strongest quality benchmarks for technical interview prep.

Strong sides:
- trusted by engineers
- deeper technical prep
- stronger credibility for serious candidates

Weak sides:
- mainly interview prep, not full career support
- less focused on job matching and portfolio generation

### StarJobs / Gitfolio

These are the most relevant niche competitors.

Strong sides:
- developer-first
- closest to your GitHub + jobs / portfolio angle

Weak sides:
- narrower products
- less complete end-to-end flow

## What your project should focus on

To stand out, focus on the parts others do not connect well:

1. GitHub-based evidence
- analyze real repos, READMEs, languages, and projects
- explain every recommendation with evidence

2. Better developer job matching
- not more listings
- fewer, higher-quality matches with reasons

3. Interview review tied to real work
- show what the user forgot to mention
- connect answers to actual projects and skills

4. Tailored outputs
- CV
- portfolio
- interview talking points

## Bottom line

Do not position this as just another mock interview app or generic resume tool.

The strongest position is:

> AI career copilot for developers, powered by real GitHub evidence.
